Eco-Tour of Paris

Sign up today for this fantastic 10-day tour of the city of light: Paris, France. For 10 days, you will enjoy the best that this wonderful city has to offer and travel secure in the knowledge that we have spared no effort to choose modes of travel and accommodation that preserve the environment and create as little waste as possible. See some of the highlights below:

Tour Eiffel (Day 1)

What visit to Paris would be complete without a indepth exploration of this magnificent feat of engineering. With special access granted only to GreenStart we'll take you behind the scenes where the general public cannot go. You'll see the tower from top to bottom in a way only a select few have ever experienced.

Paris Underground (Days 3-4)

Criss-crossing the city in all directions Paris sits on thousands of miles of tunnels and underground spaces dedicated to a host of human activities. Come with us as we explore this unique vantage of the city, ranging from the famed Metro transportation system to the 300-year-old catacombs. We'll supply the hard hat and flashlight!

Seine River Dinner Cruise (Day 6)

The Seine cuts a serpentine path through the heart of Paris dividing the city into the iconic left and right banks. You will experience the unique perspective of this beautiful city from the water's edge. Ride one of the first solar-powered, all-electric tour boats on a magical four-hour dinner cruise, serving organic, sustainable cuisine.

Champs Élysées (Day 8)

Probably the most famous street in the world and certainly one of the most beautiful. Come take an all-day walking tour of this amazing avenue. Climb to the top of the Arc de Triomphe to Napolean's private chambers. Eat lunch in a friendly street-side cafe serving local, farm-fresh meats, vegetables and cheeses. Explore the quaint and stylish boutiques. End the day at the Louvre Museum of Art, with more behind the scenes access. You will gain a completely new appreciation of Paris, guaranteed!
